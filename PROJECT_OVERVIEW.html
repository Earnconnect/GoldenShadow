In Brief

What Golden Shadow is

Golden Shadow Publishing is a two-sided platform: a curated, invitation-only marketplace where creators and executives are discovered, and a hybrid publishing studio that turns their intellectual property — talks, frameworks, podcasts, methodologies — into books, playbooks, and products they fully own.

The platform serves three audiences at once. Visitors browse a beautiful public marketplace and apply to join. Members (approved creators) get a private dashboard to manage their profile and run the AI Studio Engine. The studio team works from a secure admin back office to review applications, track revenue, and oversee every piece of AI-generated work — all without ever touching code.

Everything is built around the brand’s core promise: creators keep 100% of their IP, and the experience is “AI-assisted, human-led” — the engine drafts, the editorial team decides.

The Signature Capability

The AI Studio Engine —
your invisible infrastructure.

The engine then performs the studio’s publishing pipeline, stage by stage, producing real, ownable assets. Every output is a polished starting point your editorial team reviews and refines.

Built Right

Security, data & the stack

Security & data protection

Privacy and safety are built in, not bolted on

  • Row-level data rules — members can only ever read their own data
  • Role separation — admin areas are sealed off from regular accounts
  • Payments stay with Stripe — card details never reach the platform
  • Secret keys are server-only — never exposed to the browser
  • Secure sign-in — managed authentication for every account

The technology

Modern, proven, and ready to scale

  • Next.js + React — fast, SEO-friendly foundation
  • Supabase — secure database & accounts
  • Stripe — compliant payments & subscriptions
  • Anthropic Claude — the AI writing engine
  • Hostinger — reliable, managed hosting
